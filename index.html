About Aqua Soil

Why Choose Aqua Soil?

Aqua Soil is a scientifically designed smart substrate to maximize water retention while optimizing drainage. We use a technical mixture of silica and perlite that ensures perfect balance for your plants' health.

Our commitment is to combat global drought, reduce water waste and promote botanical conservation. Each substrate is formulated to be compatible with black soil, creating a slow-release system that efficiently feeds your plants.

From residential gardens to large reforestation projects, Aqua Soil is the ecological solution that ensures healthy, vigorous and beautiful plants in any climate.

Innovative Technology

Patented mixture of silica and perlite that guarantees optimal water and nutrient retention for your plants.

Sustainability

Eco-friendly materials that protect the environment and reduce water consumption by 30-40% compared to conventional substrates.

Botanical Compatibility

Compatible with hundreds of plant species, from decorative plants to specialized crops and reforestation projects.
